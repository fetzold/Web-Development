Bold and italicised text styling

Bold: Do not use <b>-Tag because it does not really say anything about the text. Use the <strong>-Tag.

Italicised: Do not use <i>-Tag. Use the <em>-Tag because emphasis means something more rather than just saying i.

<b>-Tag and <i>-Tag is the styling of something.

(<strong>) or emphasized (<em>) is more about the structure of it and what it means.
